Adopting a pet can be one of the most rewarding experiences of your life. Not only will you be gaining a new companion, but you'll also be helping an animal in need. Pet adoption is an important issue that deserves attention and consideration. Millions of cats and dogs end up in shelters every year in the United States alone. Adopting from a shelter helps weaken the pet overpopulation cycle.
